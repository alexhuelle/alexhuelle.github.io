Welcome to Nairobi!

After a long, pleasant flight from Berlin via Frankfort we arrive at Jomo Kenyatta Airport in Nairobi. My wife Dorothee and I enjoyed the flight, and even Lucy the cat arrives in good condition, albeit not amused.

Her mood does not improve when she learns that she is confined in a single room in the resort of the Sisters of the Holy Family. This is a quiet and green oasis, but we don’t want Lucy to go out, since she is not accustomed to that.
